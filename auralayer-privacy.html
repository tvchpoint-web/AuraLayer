Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 15, 2026

AuraLayer.ai ("we," "us," or "our") operates AuraLayer.ai. This policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights. By using AuraLayer.ai, you agree to this policy.

Information We Collect

We collect only what's necessary to deliver the service:

  • Email address — collected when you save your blend or purchase access. Used to manage your account and deliver service updates.
  • Quiz answers — your creator type, content style, tool usage, and goals. Used exclusively to generate your personalized AI blend and output.
  • Payment information — handled entirely by Stripe. We never see or store your card details.
  • Usage data — blend percentages, generation count, and session data used to improve output quality.

How We Use Your Information

  • To generate and deliver your personalized AI blend output.
  • To process one-time payments and manage your account through Stripe.
  • To send product updates and access confirmations to your email.
  • To improve the quality and relevance of AuraLayer outputs over time.

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes. Ever.

Third-Party Services

  • Stripe (stripe.com) — payment processing. Governed by Stripe's Privacy Policy.
  • Anthropic (anthropic.com) — AI output generation. Your blend data and prompt are passed to the Claude API. No data is retained by Anthropic beyond the API call.
  • Rewardful (getrewardful.com) — affiliate tracking. Referral data may be passed to track affiliate commissions.
  • Vercel (vercel.com) — hosting and serverless infrastructure.
  • Meta — advertising pixel for campaign measurement. Governed by Meta's Data Policy.

Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide the service. You may request deletion at any time by emailing support@auralayer.ai. We will respond within 10 business days.

Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal data at any time. Email us at support@auralayer.ai to exercise these rights.

Cookies

AuraLayer.ai uses only essential local storage to remember your blend, tier, and language preference during your session. We do not use third-party tracking cookies beyond the Meta pixel for ad measurement.

Children's Privacy

AuraLayer.ai is not directed at anyone under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has submitted information to us, contact us immediately at support@auralayer.ai.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Continued use of AuraLayer.ai after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

Contact
