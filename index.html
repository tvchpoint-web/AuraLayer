How it works

Three steps to your personal AI layer.

No prompting expertise required. No generic outputs. Just your tools, your weights, your results.

Step 01
✍️
Describe who you are
Tell AuraLayer who you are, what you want to accomplish, or the app you want to build. The more specific, the more personal your output.
Step 02
⚡
Select your tools & set influence
Choose up to 5 AI tools from the matrix. Assign each one a weighted percentage — 35% Claude, 25% Canva, 20% Notion. Lock percentages you want to protect.
Step 03
✦
Activate your Aura
AuraLayer interprets your blend and generates a fully personalized output — guidance, opportunities, workflows, or a complete app blueprint — shaped by your exact tool DNA.
