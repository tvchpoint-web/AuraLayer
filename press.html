✦   The Story

Most creators use four or five AI tools in a single workflow. Claude for writing. Canva for visuals. Perplexity for research. ChatGPT for ideation. They tab-switch constantly, copy-paste outputs between platforms, and end up with content that sounds like it was written by a committee of robots.

AuraLayer solves this by letting you set the percentage weight of each tool in your blend. 40% Claude, 35% Canva, 25% Perplexity. Hit generate. The output sounds like you — not like AI slop.
